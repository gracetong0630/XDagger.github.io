Community fund rewards for July

08-06
2018

Dear community, the rewards for the month of July 2018 have been decided.

The rewards are based on Github contributions and notable community involvement. Transaction IDs are linked to each name. The amounts reflect a rough average value of the currency in the last 5 days of the month.
